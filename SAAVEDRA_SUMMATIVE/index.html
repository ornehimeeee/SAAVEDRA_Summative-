Excellence and Virtue

Mapúa Malayan Colleges is a premier educational institution in the Philippines, renowned for its excellence in engineering, business, and technology programs. With campuses in Laguna and Mindanao, it extends the legacy of Mapúa University by offering globally competitive education tailored to regional development. The college emphasizes innovation, integrity, and industry alignment, preparing students for real-world challenges through immersive learning and strategic partnerships.

Effective Learning Spaces

By fusing innovative physical learning spaces and digital learning environments, Mapúa MCM provides an inspiring environment to study, learn, and collaborate with peers. As a technological school in Davao City, we provide WiFi inside the campus, cutting-edge learning resources, and a robust digital learning management system.
