About Us

Our Legacy

Mapúa Malayan Colleges Mindanao (MMCM) is a premier educational institution located in Davao City, Philippines. Established in 2010, MMCM is part of the Mapúa University system, known for its excellence in engineering and technology education. The college offers a range of undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields, including engineering, information technology, business, and architecture.

Quality Education

MMCM is committed to providing quality education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills. The institution boasts state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty members, and a vibrant campus life that fosters holistic development among its students. MMCM also emphasizes research and innovation, encouraging students and faculty to engage in projects that address real-world challenges.

Outreach and Charity

With a strong focus on community engagement and sustainability, MMCM actively participates in outreach programs and initiatives that contribute to the betterment of society. The college aims to produce graduates who are not only competent professionals but also socially responsible citizens ready to make a positive impact in their respective fields.
