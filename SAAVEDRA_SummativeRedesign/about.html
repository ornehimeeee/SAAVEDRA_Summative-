Our History

Mapúa Malayan Colleges Mindanao, established in 2015, is a branch that brings Mapúa University's legacy of technological and academic excellence to Mindanao. The institution officially commenced operations on July 2, 2018, followed by its formal inauguration ceremony on July 7, 2018.
